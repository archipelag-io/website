About Archipelag.io

One-liner

Archipelag.io is a distributed compute network that routes AI workloads to nearby community-operated hardware.

Short description

Archipelag.io connects people who need compute with people who already have it. Users submit AI workloads, and the network routes them to a nearby host: someone's gaming PC, workstation, or phone. The host runs the job in a sandboxed container, streams results back, and earns credits for the work. A coordinator service handles placement, dispatch, and billing.

Extended description

Archipelag.io is a distributed compute network where anyone can contribute GPU, CPU, or neural engine resources and earn credits, while users get AI inference served from hardware that might be down the street rather than in a data center three time zones away. The platform supports LLM chat, image generation, and general inference workloads, with hosts ranging from gaming PCs with discrete GPUs to iPhones running small models on-device. A reputation system called karma tracks host reliability, and a compute exchange sets prices through supply and demand. The network is currently in open beta, with all credits and earnings virtual through June 2026.
