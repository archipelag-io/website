05 — FAQ

Common questions

How does jurisdiction routing work?

When you submit a Cargo, you specify a jurisdiction policy (e.g., EU-West, Switzerland). The platform routes your job exclusively to verified nodes within that jurisdiction. Your data never leaves the specified region during processing.

What are verification levels?

Compute providers go through three verification tiers. Tier 1: identity + hardware attestation for standard Cargos. Tier 2: facility audit + compliance training for GDPR Cargos. Tier 3: dedicated infrastructure + SLA for enterprise and regulated Cargos.

Is Archipelag.io GDPR compliant?

Yes. The platform is designed for GDPR compliance from the ground up. Jurisdiction routing ensures data residency, verified nodes provide processing guarantees, and complete audit trails document every execution for regulatory reporting.

Are you SOC 2 certified?

SOC 2 certification is on our Q4 2026 roadmap. Enterprise tier customers will have access to our compliance documentation and can request audit reports as we progress through certification.

How does this help with the EU AI Act?

Archipelag.io helps teams meet AI Act requirements through verified execution, audit trails, and transparency reporting. Our compliance toolkit (Q4 2026) will include AI Act-specific templates and documentation for regulatory submissions.

Is the beta really free?

Yes. The beta uses virtual credits — no real money changes hands. Nobody pays, nobody gets paid. You get 10,000 credits on signup and they auto-refill. We may reset data before launch.

How will pricing work after the beta?

Pricing will be market-based. Islands set asking prices, consumers set bid preferences, and the compute exchange clears a rate per Cargo type. No fixed tiers, no subscriptions. You buy credits when you need them.

Do credits expire?

No. Credits never expire. Use them whenever you need them.

Do I need to verify my identity?

Identity verification (government ID + selfie) is required before submitting Cargos or registering as an Island. You can browse the Cargo Registry and explore the platform without verification. We use Stripe Identity for secure verification and only store your verified name and country.
