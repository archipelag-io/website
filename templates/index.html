04 — Platform

Built for sovereignty from day one

Jurisdiction Routing

Route workloads to specific jurisdictions. EU, Switzerland, or custom regions. Your data stays where you say.

Node Verification

Every compute provider is verified. Identity attestation, hardware checks, and compliance certification.

Policy Enforcement

Define compliance policies in code. GDPR, AI Act, SOC 2. Policies are enforced at the infrastructure level.
