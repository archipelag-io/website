Why Archipelag.io

Built for sovereignty from day one

Jurisdiction Routing

Route workloads to specific jurisdictions. EU, Switzerland, or custom regions. Your data stays where you say.

Node Verification

Every compute provider is verified. Identity attestation, hardware checks, and compliance certification.

Policy Enforcement

Define compliance policies in code. GDPR, AI Act, SOC 2. Policies are enforced at the infrastructure level.

Data Residency

Data never leaves your specified region. Encrypted in transit, processed locally, no cross-border transfers.

Audit Trail

Complete execution logs for every workload. Who processed it, where, when, and how. Ready for regulatory review.

Compliance-First

Designed for regulated industries. Healthcare, finance, government. Sovereignty is not an afterthought.
